A step for a change

A Step for Change In light of the UN COP27 Conference, HHC organized a climate awareness drive, “Step for a Change,” which included our members walking 5km in the neighborhood and holding up posters advocating climate change and Call to Action. This event was a representation of our sincere commitment towards a better and sustainable future for the coming generations and an advocate of the growing depletion in resources and bioenergy. By engaging our community and raising awareness, we hope to inspire collective action towards environmental conservation and sustainable practices.
