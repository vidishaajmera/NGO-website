Teen Tech Tutor

Teen Tech Tutors is a project where members help senior citizens learn technological skills that not only increases their technology literacy to enable them to perform activities more efficiently but also evokes empathy in the members. As a part of TTT, our members are carrying out this activity in their buildings and neighborhoods. We plan to carry out TTT on a larger scale impacting more seniors by conducting the sessions at old age homes.

One of the biggest advantages would be to make some of their daily chores easier and a significant reduction in time spent by learning Digital banking and using Ecommerce apps for grocery and occasional food ordering. We are grateful for having been able to make an impact on our elders who are shapers of our society.
