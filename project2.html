Women in STEM Interview Series

In today's world, where STEM is becoming increasingly prevalent, we are connecting with women professionals to bring their experiences and insights to the forefront. This series is designed to inspire the next generation of young women to pursue careers in STEM fields.

Through interviews and discussions, we highlight the challenges and triumphs these professionals have faced in their careers. Our aim is to provide role models for young women and encourage them to follow their passion in STEM.
